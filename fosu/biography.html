Our Patron

Adedayo Peter Sunday (APS) Arogundade

Managing Director, Sidmach Technology.


APS is a co-founder of Nigeria leading ICT Company, driving development and innovation in the technology industry. He is currently the managing Director of Sidmach Technology, a company that delivers an array of tech solution that makes works faster and life easier.


APS as he is fondly called is a well-respected alumnus of University of Lagos; he is also decorated with numerous certificates in ICT, Management and executive Leadership both locally and overseas. He has served in different top level position ranging from executive Director Finance and Admin, to Executive Director Sales and Marketing, Executive Director ICT infrastructure, and Executive Director of Software Solution.


APS dexterity as a leader has led Sidmach to becoming Nigeria leading tech-solution provider In education, Health, Finance and Agriculture; partnering with globally recognized brands like Microsoft, HP, Sophos among others, In his position as Managing Director of Sidmach, the company has achieved back to back recognition in the Nigeria software industry.


In 2007, Sidmach was award the “Best Indigenous Software Company of the year” and “Technology information Hub of the Year”, whilst the Managing Director was himself conferred with the award of the “ICT Development towards the Development of Nigeria, Africa, Continent and the Entire world, in Accra Ghana”. He also led Sidmach to being recognized in 2017 as one of the “Most Innovative in Education” by Technology Africa, during its “Titan of Technology Award” in 2016. As recent as November 16, 2018 he won the award of “I.T Personality of the year” at National Information Technology Merit Awards 2018.


APS is a distinguished Fellow of Nigeria Computer Society of Nigeria (FNCS); Fellow, Institute of Management Consultant and Fellow, Association of Corporate Governance Professional of Nigeria. He is also a Member Institute of Director (MIOD). He always Encourage and promote Youth Education and Empowerment. He chair the APS Youth Foundation and the activities of the foundation has led Elder APS Arogundade to bag various Recognition and merit awards.


Chief APS Arogundade, a community leader, lover of the youths, national development contributor and man of integrity. He's happily married and blessed with children.

Our Matron

Lade Ade-Ibileke FCA, ACTI, FCAI MBA, HND Accountancy

She is happily married to Elder Joseph Ade Ibileke and blessed with Children


Ogidi development association started in year 2005 as a result of disagreement with the then Odu national president Barrister femi Oloruntoba and some group of ilaere elite under the leadership of Mr.Tunde Ipinmisho. The later group allegedly converge to convey the National Annual General Meeting of the Union in absence of both the president and the secretary Mr.Ade Ibileke. Although, earlier before then, the issue of Ologidi stool rotation which has linger since about the year 1942 between Ono ilaere and Onogogongo came into being as a result of vacancy created by the death of Oba Are Jegede.


The emergen of ODA gave a boost to the fight and struggle for Ologidi stool rotation by involving the Otun clan of Ogidi into the struggle which eventually saw the ODA sponsoring late Oba Joseph Alamoh as a candidate for Ologidi against the present Ologidi Oba Oladimeji Sule.The contest was hot and the ever best synergy to rescue Ogidiland from perpetual dominance of Oke Ilaere and Itaji chieftancy family. The president of ODA from inception to date is Mr O.S Thomas. Under his leadership ODA branches were established across the country with a view of enlightens our people to struggle for a common front on tripod stands of rotation of Ologidi stool , equal rights for all Ogidi citizens and selection of kingmaker.


The Association register it's youths vanguard federation of ogidi students union and has since inception continually foster unity among its members. The struggle for ologidi has never been competitive as in the present scenario even our opponent are aware and accept this fact. The Association has embarked on so many community projects like purchase of an electric transformer, renovation of ogidi community secondary school, sponsoring of our youths to institutions of higher learning and gainful employment.


The Association is sponsoring presently and got Approval for ogidi police station and to replace all electric poles on high tension wires within its jurisdiction in Ogidiland. The Association on every 26th Dec. organize an Annual General Meeting for all members across the nation with a seminar. The Association is well recognized among community Association in Kogi State. The Association has met severally with opposition groups in ogidi and outside Ogidi with a view of uniting and reconciling but it's proposal on rotation of Ologidi stool and selection of kingmaker has not be accepted by the other group.

Who We Are

Federation of Ogidi Student Union goal is to protect overall interest of all Ogidi students(FOSU) when necessary and to work in collaboration with all the indigene of Ogidi land (Home and abroad) for economic, social, cultural and educational development of Ogidi land.
