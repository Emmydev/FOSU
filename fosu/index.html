About Us

The name FOSU is an acronym for Federation of Ogidi student union. FOSU became a registered union in 2007. The Union was formed for the purpose of promoting justice and equity within students in Ogidi kingdom...Read more

Our Activities

Annual football competition

Football competition is one of the activities of FOSU. The competition was named after Chief APS Arogundade.

Annual Congress

The annual congress usually comes up ones in a year during the festive period, which include election of new executives and handing over.

Beauty Pageant Night

The beauty pageant night (Miss Fosu) usually comes up every 28th of December.

Tutorial, Quiz and Debate

We organize tutorial class, quiz and debate competion for secondary school students.

Who We Are

Federation of Ogidi Student Union goal is to protect overall interest of all Ogidi students(FOSU) when necessary and to work in collaboration with all the indigene of Ogidi land (Home and abroad) for economic, social, cultural and educational development of Ogidi land.
