About UsFederation of Ogidi Student Union

What are we all about?

The name FOSU is an acronym for Federation of Ogidi student union. FOSU became a registered union in 2007. The Union was formed for the purpose of promoting justice and equity within students in Ogidi kingdom.


The name FOSU was given by Mr Dele Kirin been backedup by Mr OS Thomas (ODA President), Barr. Femi Oloruntoba, Otunba APS Arogundade, Mr Segun Ogbonnewo, Mr Tunde Balogun, Mr Ade Ibileke and host of others elite that believed in equal and balance rationale in the struggle of brotherhood.


Femi Ibileke took the mantle of acting President in 2008 for some few months and handed over to Sunday Bature in the same year. Thereafter, Abiodun Ayodele was officially elected as the first president of the Union in 2009.


The goal Federation of Ogidi Student Union is to protect overall interest of all Ogidi students(FOSU) when necessary and to work in collaboration with all the indigene of Ogidi land (Home and abroad) for economic, social, cultural and educational development of Ogidi land.


FOSU has been in existence to foster unity among Ogidi Ela students.

Mode of Election

  • To be elected to any post, the contestant must be a student of any recognized higher institution with evidence (Admission letter/School ID card)
  • He/She must be an indigene of the community(Ogidi)
  • He/She must be screened in alliance with the constitution
